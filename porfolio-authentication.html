Coffee App

Coffee Shop

Authentication and Authorization is an important consideration when it comes to App development. We developers have different ideas when it comes to providing the top security for our Application. It makes sense to treat our authentication service as a microservice of its own; i.e., have our authentication service acting as a single self-contained unit to handle everything related to authentication. With this project I made use of a third party authentication service AUTH0.

From a developers point of View passwords are the worst, because our users will introduce a tremendous amount of risk by not understanding how to use passwords securely, and also developers don't take them serious enough and miss simple things that opens our system to attack

Within the scope of this project I was able to accomplish handling routes that authorize and authenticate a user to access certain points based on the permission policy been granted.
